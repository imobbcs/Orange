# When to Sell Bitcoin? A Simple Guide

Many people get into Bitcoin without knowing when or if they should ever sell. This article helps you stay on track and find your strategy – without stress, without FOMO.

## Why sell at all?

Bitcoin is a long-term investment. But sometimes there are good reasons to sell:

- You need money for a major purchase
- Your risk tolerance has changed
- You want to realize profits

### Emotions versus strategy

Avoid selling out of fear or greed. Clear rules help:

- Set a target: e.g., partial sale at +100% profit
- Use fixed time points: e.g., review every January

## Practical tips for timing your sale

- Keep an eye on the market, but not hourly
- Use tools like whentobuybtc.xyz for guidance
- Automate partial sales with fixed rules

## Conclusion

There's no "perfect timing." But with a clear strategy and clear head, you can make informed decisions.
