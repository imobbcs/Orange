When the Fear & Greed Index falls into extreme fear territory — as it is now — experienced long-term Bitcoin holders tend to see it as an opportunity rather than a warning. Historically, the periods of deepest fear have aligned closely with local price bottoms. Many DCA investors choose to increase their regular purchase amount during these windows, rather than reduce or pause it. The logic is straightforward: if your conviction in Bitcoin's long-term trajectory hasn't changed, then a lower price is simply a better entry point.
